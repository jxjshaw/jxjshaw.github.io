inspiration work me
1

Pragmatism

For me, design is the pursuit of instrumental value. It is the creation of a semi-perfect outcome, whatever it takes, instead of the following of any specific process. There was a certain moment in my life when I grew tired of asking the world more questions. Instead I became more interseted in constructing answers. That was the day I knew I was going to pursue design for the rest of my life.

2

New
perspectives

The moments I live for are refreshing and magical. Yet I also believe deeply in the timelessness of the human condition. So another design value of mine is to merely revisit these fundamental activities, but in ways that have never been done before. My favorite designs use the latest technology to help us see the same world in new ways.

3

essence

Another fundamental value of mine is truth. I'd rather see the world for what it is, and without ornamentation. Whether the truth is beautiful or ugly, i'm not the type that covers things up. For me, transparency is the purest form of refinement: flat hierarchies, raw content, and exposed structures.

4

tension and
harmony

Across any form or modality, the language that ties all perceivable things together for me is tension and harmony. It speaks to a human tendency to understand everything as a story, and the need to assign it an ending. For any artifact, there is some amount of white space, silence, or opposing force that feels like the right amount. Most of the time that aesthetic balance is discovered by chance and easily lost, but I believe an effective designer should have the necessary tools and process to find their way back quickly each time, regardless of the medium.
