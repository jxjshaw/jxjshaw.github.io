I grew up in rural minnesota hanging out at the gas station, working on cars, buildings rafts, playing world of warcraft. Life started to make more sense after coming across Plato and the Big Lebowski. I studied philosophy in school, but it was in design where I found the practice of humanities.

For fun I like learning things like bass and piano. I find peace in harmony, and beauty in tension. Music has taught me how to listen, to trust my intuition and embrace mistakes.

The best days of my life are spontaneous. I love staying up late, building stuff, learning new perspectives, making new friends. To me these are the ingredients that make life magical.
